About

I am currently an assistant professor in Radboud University at the AI department and a visiting assistant professor at the Yale School of Management. My main research focus is on multiagent interaction, complex systems and reinforcement learning. Before coming to Academia I help several postions in Tech and in Trading and in startups.

Research Interests

  • Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning
  • Complex Systems and Emergent Behaviors
  • Chemical AI and Molecular Design
  • Game-Theoretic Approaches in AI
  • LLM Multi-Agent Systems
  • Market Microstructure
  • Cryptography and Lattice based Cryptography
  • Cryptocurrency and Digital Trading
