Research

Complex Systems

Investigating the dynamics of interconnected systems and emergent collective behaviors. I explore how simple interaction rules give rise to sophisticated system-level phenomena, with applications to distributed intelligence and adaptive networks.

Chemical AI

Developing machine learning methods at the interface of chemistry and artificial intelligence. This work focuses on using AI to predict molecular properties, design novel compounds, and understand chemical reaction mechanisms.

Game Theoretical Scenarios Using Deep Learning

Applying deep reinforcement learning to game theoretic problems. I study strategic interactions in multi-agent environments, equilibrium concepts, and the emergence of cooperative and competitive behaviors in learned policies.

LLM and Multi-Agent Interactions

Exploring how large language models interact in multi-agent settings. This research examines communication protocols, coordination mechanisms, and emergent linguistic behaviors when multiple language models collaborate or compete.
